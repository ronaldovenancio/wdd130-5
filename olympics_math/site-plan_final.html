Overview

Purpose

The Mathematics is a science extremely important for everyone and your Olympic Math is one of the most prestigious science olympic competition in the world. In Brazil, there are many differents of Olympics Mathematics for specifics level of students, like OBM, OBMEP and OMU.

Audience

This is a competition for students in the 6th to 9th year of elementary school and all grades of high school in public and private schools in Brazil. The competition consists of a test carried out at poles defined by the coordination in a single phase or in two phases.

This guide have the intention to help secondary and high schools students that search about Olympics Mathematics Competition in Brazil to plan your studies in order to participate in some Olympics Mathematics.
